About Me

Hello, my name is Jess. As of Spring '23, I am a 3rd year AGPM major. Interests include digital art, tabletop roleplaying games, video games, and writing.

I tend to do quietly do things on my own, but become a bit more social when someone prompts me for interactions.

Current personal projects of mine include the Twine game Limbo, the Google Docs-Twine hybrid Navigation, and the visual novel Ruin.
